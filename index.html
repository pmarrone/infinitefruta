Route Loop!

Vote for us at http://globalgamejam.org/2012/route-loop

Watch the gameplay video at http://youtu.be/kYEFAB1BS7E
  • Drag pieces of road into empty spaces by clicking and dragging them with your mouse.
  • Build a road to keep the car moving! If the car goes out of track, you lose the round.
  • As you play, you will lose gas and gain speed.
    • Refill your tank by picking up the gas pumps. (required to beat a level)
    • Lose speed by picking up snails (picking these up is entirely optional)
  • When you pick enough gas pumps as indicated in the UI, the barrier will go up.
  • When the barrier is up, guide the car to the starting point and prepare for the next level!
  • There are 4 levels right now.
